## **Compliance Considerations: CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL**

Although Egypt does not yet enforce strict consumer email marketing laws like GDPR, businesses sending emails internationally must adhere to compliance frameworks when interacting with global inbox providers or foreign recipients.

### **Key Laws to Respect in Outreach**

| Regulation | Applies When | Key Requirement |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **GDPR** (EU) | Targeting EU residents from Egypt | Consent or legitimate interest, opt-out required |
| **CAN-SPAM** (US) | Emailing US-based systems or users | Clear unsubscribe, no misleading headers |
| **CASL** (Canada) | Messaging Canadian recipients | Consent \+ identification required |

### **Reference:**

* CAN-SPAM Act (FTC): https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/can-spam-act-compliance-guide

Snippet:  
 “Marketing emails must always provide a clear opt-out, valid sender identity, and no deceptive subject lines.”
